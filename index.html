The BMW E30, produced from 1982 to 1994, is a classic compact executive car that is highly regarded among enthusiasts. Part of the BMW 3 Series, it features a distinctive boxy design and rear-wheel drive, offering a range of engines, from economical four-cylinders to powerful six-cylinder options. The E30 M3, in particular, is celebrated for its high-revving 2.3-liter inline-four engine and motorsport heritage. Known for its balanced handling and solid build quality, the E30 remains a beloved classic, with a strong aftermarket scene for restoration and modification. Its combination of sportiness and practicality continues to attract admirers today.
